## Description

FocusPing is not another planner. It is a delivery gate for your brain.

If you have ADHD, you know the problem: reminders fire at 9am while you are in hyperfocus, or tasks vanish at midnight when you did not finish them. Planner apps punish you for being human.

FocusPing does the opposite.

**Hold during Focus**
Pings wait while you are in system Focus or Deep Work. No wrong-time interruptions.

**I'm ready**
When you have capacity to act, one tap delivers everything waiting in your queue.

**No guilt design**
No red overdue badges. No streaks. No tasks that disappear at midnight. Missed pings stay in a safe queue until you are ready.

**Works with your stack**
Import from Apple Reminders. Add pings with Siri. Home screen widget and Live Activity. Snooze 15 minutes to 3 hours.

**Built for ADHD adults**
Quick templates for meds, water, messages, and more. Search your pings. Undo accidental deletes. Delivery window for quiet hours.

FocusPing: remind you when you can act.
